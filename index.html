social Health care app

What is
XaraCare?

XaraCare is a unique interactive platform that connects women to the most trusted care in Africa. The social care app allows women to get access to care services/products, discover tips, learn and get support in real time.

Benefits

Get Support
24/7

⌾Women have the opportunity to access care experts and get support from the xaracare community.

⌾Service providers have the possibility to offer their services in a convenient way.

How it works

Service
Providers

Care experts who are willing to provide their services are given the possibility to create searchable online profiles on the app.

How it works

Consumers

The xaracare platform creates semi-anonymous profile to each user who is registered on the app. Users can search for care experts to get advice/services ranging from health, beauty and fitness. Our e-communities allow users to interact with each other by making post and comments .

Main Features

E-communities

Each user registered on the app can join e-communities of their choice to interact with other users in the app.

Create Posts /Ask questions

Users can make posts/ask questions in the communities and comment on others posts and questions.

Contact Experts

Users can have a live chat or book appointments with care experts in real time.
